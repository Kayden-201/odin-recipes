Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that is a staple in many households.It is made with layers of pasta, meat or vegetables, and a rich and creamy sauce, all baked to perfection in the oven.

This dish is hearty and comforting, making it the perfect meal for a cold winter night. The possibilities for customization are endless, from a classic meat sauce to a vegetarian option, and from a traditional ricotta filling to a spinach and feta version.

The end result is always a dish that is both delicious and satisfying. Perfect for a family dinner or a special occassion, lasagna is a dish that will never disappoint

As stated above, this is a highly customizable dish and hence, the ingredients listed may be altered to suit the target eater&rsquos needs.

As good as it is, it is worthy to note that your&squoe going to be waiting aroung awhile to enjoy it, for as the saying goes, &ldquoGood things come to those who wait.&rdquo

Ingredients

  • Meat (ground beef or Italian sausage>
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • sugar
  • Water
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Fresh parsley
  • Dried basil leaves
  • Salt
  • Italian seasoning
  • Fennel seeds
  • Ground black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Egg
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozarella chesse
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C)
  2. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the grund beef or sausage until browned. Drain any excess grease.
  3. Add the onion and garlic to the skilletand cook until softened.
  4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce and water.
  5. Add sugar, basil, fennel seeds, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  6. Bring the mixture to a simmer and let it cook for about 10 minutes.
  7. Cook the lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water. (Alternative, cook lasagna according to package instructions).
  8. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg and chopped parsley.
  9. To assemble the lasagna, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  10. Pllace a layer of cooked lasagna on top of the sauce.
  11. Spread a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.
  12. Sprinkle a layer of mozarella and parmesan cheese over the ricotta mixture.
  13. Repeat layers until allingredients are used up, making sure to end with a layer of meat sauce and cheeses on top.
  14. Cover the lasagna with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  15. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25-30 minutes, or until cheese on top is glden brown and bubbly.
  16. Allow the lasagna to cool for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving
