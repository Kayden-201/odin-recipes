Shrimp Curry

Description

Shrimp curry is a delicious and flavorful dish that is perfect for a comforting weeknight meal. This recipe is a classic South Asian-inspired dish that is made with succulent shrimp, cooked in a rich and fragrant curry sauce. The curry is made with a blend of aromatic spices such as cumin, coriander, turmeric, and garam masala, which provide a depth of flavor that is hard to beat.

The dish is easy to make, and it can be prepared in just a few steps. First, the onions, ginger, and garlic are sauteed until golden brown, which gives the dish a wonderful base flavor. Then, the tomatoes, coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chilli powder, and garam masala powder are added and cooked until the tomatoes are soft and the oil starts to separate from the masala. Finally, the shrimp is added, and the curry is simmered until the shrimp is cooked through.

The recipe is versatile and can be adjusted to suit your taste preferences. You can add more or less spice to make it mild or hot, and you can also add additional ingredients such as potatoes, carrots, and green peas for variation.

This shrimp curry is a perfect dish to serve with steamed rice, naan, or roti. It is sure to be a crowd-pleaser, and it is sure to become a regular in your dinner rotation.

Ingredients

  • Shrimp (peeled and deveined)
  • Onions
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Ginger (minced)
  • Finely chopped chilli peppers (optional)
  • Finely chopped tomatoes
  • Vegetable oil
  • Cumin seeds
  • Coriander powder
  • Turmeric powder
  • Red chilli powder (optional)
  • Garam masala powder
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Fresh chopped cilantro

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pan or pot over medium heat.
  2. Add cumin seeds and cook until they start to sizzle.
  3. Add onions, ginger, and garlic and cook until the onions are golden brown.
  4. Add the tomatoes, coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chilli powder (if using), and garam masala powder. Cook until the tomatoes are soft and the oil starts to separate from the masala.
  5. Add the shrimp and stir to coat with the masala. Cook until the shrimp turns pink (or opaque).
  6. Add enough water to cover the shrimp and bring the curry to a boil.
  7. Reduce the heat and let the curry simmer, covered, for about 5-10 minutes or until the shrimp is cooked through.
  8. Adjust the seasoning with salt to taste.
  9. Garnish with fresh chopped cilantro.
